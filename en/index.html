In development

ProPonte

Live — closed testing

A public-procurement opportunity tracker for Colombian companies. ProPonte continuously monitors SECOP — Colombia's official procurement system (SECOP I & II) — and surfaces the government tenders and bidding notices that match your business.

Offline-first by design: your company profile — sector (UNSPSC), region and contract-value range — is stored and filtered on your own device, and you get an alert the moment a matching opportunity is published. Free, ad-supported, and independent from any government entity.
