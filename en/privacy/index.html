Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This is the privacy policy for renovatiosoftware.net, the website of Renovatio Software, an independent software studio run by Camilo Meneses (Colombia). It explains what this website does — and does not — do with your data, and how to reach us.

This website collects no personal data

renovatiosoftware.net is a static informational website. It has no sign-up, no accounts, no forms, no analytics and no advertising or tracking cookies. We do not build profiles of visitors and we do not sell or share any visitor data — because we do not collect any.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare. Like any web host, these providers may process standard technical request data (such as IP address and browser type) in their own server logs to deliver the site and protect it from abuse, under their respective privacy policies. Renovatio Software does not receive or store this information.

Contact

If you email us, we use your message and address only to reply and to keep a record of our correspondence. You can reach us at either address below:

Primary support@renovatiosoftware.net
Alternative renovatiossoftware@gmail.com

Please use the primary address when possible; the alternative is a backup channel.

Our apps have their own policies

Products we publish handle data under their own privacy policies, separate from this website. For our procurement-tracking app ProPonte, see the ProPonte data policy. Several of our apps are offline-first, keeping your data on your device.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Questions about it can go to either contact address.
