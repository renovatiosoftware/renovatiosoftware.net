Common questions

How do I back up my collection?
In Settings → Export, Setvault creates a single encrypted .setvault file. Save it wherever you trust — your phone, an external drive, or a cloud folder you control. Basic export is always free.
How do I restore a backup or move to a new phone?
Install Setvault on the new device and use Settings → Import, then choose your .setvault file. Everything comes back, photos included. You'll be asked for the passphrase you set when exporting.
I paid for Pro but it isn't showing — how do I restore it?
Use Restore purchase in Settings, signed in with the same Google account you bought it with. Setvault confirms your Pro status directly with Google Play. If it still doesn't appear, email us with your order number.
Which coins and series are included?
Setvault starts with United States series such as the Lincoln Wheat Cent (1909–1958), Jefferson Nickel, Mercury Dime and the 50 State Quarters. More series are on the way — tell us which one you'd like next.
Is my data safe if I lose my phone?
Your collection lives encrypted on the device, so keeping a recent .setvault backup is what protects you. With that file you can restore everything on a new phone. We can't recover data for you, because we never have it.
I have a feature request or found a bug.
We'd love to hear it. Email support@renovatiosoftware.net with a short description (and a screenshot if it's a bug). Requests from collectors shape what we build next.
