Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 16, 2026

Setvault collects nothing. No account, no cloud, no analytics, no advertising — your collection, photos and notes stay encrypted on your device, and never reach us or anyone else.

What we collect

Nothing. Setvault has no servers that receive your data. We do not create an account for you, we do not track your usage, and we do not run analytics or advertising SDKs inside the app.

Your collection and photos

Everything you add — coins, photos, purchase prices and notes — is stored in an encrypted database on your device (SQLCipher). Your photos are the ones you take of your own coins; they stay on the device and are never uploaded.

Backups

When you choose to back up, Setvault creates a single .setvault file that you save wherever you decide — your phone storage, a drive, or a cloud folder of your choice. We never receive that file. Restoring it brings your collection back on any device.

Purchases

The one-time Pro unlock is processed by Google Play billing. Google handles the payment; we never see your card or payment details. Your Pro status is confirmed by the store, not by any data we hold.

Third parties

Setvault contains no third-party analytics, no advertising networks and no social trackers. The only external party involved is Google Play, and only for downloading the app and processing the optional Pro purchase.

Children

Setvault is a general-audience app for coin collectors and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date. Because Setvault collects no data, changes are expected to be minimal.
